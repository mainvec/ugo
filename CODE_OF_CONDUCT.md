# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free community.
Examples of positive behavior include constructive feedback, empathy,
professional disagreement, and respect for different experiences and
viewpoints.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized
attention, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, or publishing
another person's private information without permission.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing
the project, including issues, pull requests, reviews, and related community
discussions.

## Enforcement

Report abusive content using GitHub's reporting tools and contact a project
maintainer through the contact methods on their GitHub profile for
project-specific enforcement. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and
may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates
this policy.

Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.
Retaliation against a reporter is itself a violation of this policy.
